  • Use these colors most of the time.

  • Other than text, avoid black.

Borders and Corners

  • Round sharp corners most of the time.
  • Use a small radius, something like 4px
  • Avoid 1px lines and borders.
  • Use 3 or 4px lines most of the time.

Fonts

  • Headlines and body copy in Josefin Sans from Google fonts.
  • Two weights: Copy 300 and Headlines 400.
